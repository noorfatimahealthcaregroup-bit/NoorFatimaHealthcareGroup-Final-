Who We Are

The story of Noor Fatima Healthcare Group began in 2010, when Dr. Qaisar War opened a small retail pharmacy with determination, compassion, and a vision for change. At that time, his mission was simple yet profound — to make reliable medicines and essential healthcare accessible to every family, regardless of their background.

What started as a modest store soon became more than just a business. It became a promise of trust. Patients began to see not only a place to buy medicines, but a place where they were heard, cared for, and treated with dignity. Dr. War’s vision turned into a community effort, one step at a time, fueled by a belief that healthcare is not a privilege, but a right for all.

As the years went by, that single pharmacy grew into something larger — clinics, diagnostic centers, and pharmacies under one banner, all united by the same values that shaped its foundation. Each milestone was a reflection of our core principle: compassion first, quality always.

Today, Noor Fatima Healthcare Group is proud to serve thousands of patients across multiple communities. While we continue to expand, our foundation remains unchanged — a dedication to affordable access, trusted care, and a patient-first approach. What began as one man’s vision has now become a growing healthcare family, committed to improving lives with integrity and compassion.
